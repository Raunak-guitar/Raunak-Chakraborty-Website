RAUNAK CHAKRABORTY
Raunak Chakraborty is a guitarist from Bay Area. He had started his journey three years ago and has built his way up so far. He has played guitar with many different bands and people across the bay area including his band, Udaan which is a bengali band that does rock, folk, and classical music. He has many shows across the bay area and everyone knows him as Electriying Figo. He plays guitar on many different varying genres although his main genre is hard rock. He has met many people in the industry and he met and seen professional bands. He is also a professional music artist releasing music on streaming platforms. He released his first single dreamland back in May and he also composes music a lot. He's pretty popular in the Bay Area and is trying to make guitar his profession.
